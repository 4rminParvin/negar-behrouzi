About Me

Hi. I’m Negar.
I have been tremendously passionate about art my whole life. I enjoy creating and designing wholeheartedly. Nothing has been able to reduce my enthusiasm so far.
When it comes to designing, I’m a hard-working, energetic person who is eager to learn new skills and further expand her knowledge constantly.
I have always regarded punctuality as a crucial trait for me to have; therefore, I’d say that I’m great at time management and being loyal to deadlines.
