Hi. I'm Negar.
I have been tremendously passionate about art my whole life. I enjoy creating and designing wholeheartedly. Nothing has been able to reduce my enthusiasm so far.
When it comes to designing, I'm a hard-working, energetic person who is eager to learn new skills and further expand her knowledge constantly.
I have always regarded punctuality as a crucial trait for me to have; therefore, I'd say that I'm great at time management and being loyal to deadlines.


Portfolio

T-Shirt Design Based on a Novel; Kafka on the Shore

Kafka on the Shore is one of Haruki Murakami's most famous books. The story is so long and when you keep reading it, a deep relationship will be shaped between you and the characters. I really loved this Novel, so I decided to design some T-Shirts based on it. Luckily, some other fans got excited and bought them.

Sycamore Accessories Logo Design

Sycamore is an online shop that sells accessories. I designed their logo at the beginning of their work! As Sycamore is the name of a tree, I used Sycamore tree's leaf in my design. So we have a ring and a leaf in the logo.

Brand Design and Logo Redesign for Armitaje

I started working at Armitaje as a graphic designer in April. Armitaje is a growing and developing fashion brand in the US and I hope we publish our works soon. I redesigned their logo, designed their brand and packaging, and also prepared some designs on computer to perform for other parts of the company.
